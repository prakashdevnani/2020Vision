Outgoing Kalam outlines 2020 vision for India

Published: Monday, July 23, 2007, 20:03 [IST]
New Delhi, July 23: Emphasising the need for having a vision for the nation among the Members of Parliament, outgoing President A P J Abdul Kalam in his farewell speech here today called for the future political leadership to rise to the global challenges of development and sustainability of growth. Addressing the MPs at the Central hall of the Parliament, where he was today given a READ MORE farewell by the Members of the Parliament, Kalam said: "The future political leadership the world over has to rise to the challenge of sustainability of growth, development, environment and resources." "The national leadership has to radiate confidence in our people that 'we can do it'," added Kalam, who also outlined his 2020 vision for the country to the MPs.
